May 30 - Summer Book Festival

May 13, 2015
It's coming up.. ..and it's bigger and better than ever before!  It is the annual Summer Book Festival and I'm super excited.  There is going to be music, food, dancing, face painting, balloon animals, activities for everyone, a performance by the hit band The Str!ke, and 10,000 books to give away.  Plus, it is free for the whole family.  Well, I guess there is a cost to get in-reading!  The summer is not a time to stop expanding your imagination by not reading books; it is time to read even more.  I love to go to the book festival AND my mom loves it, too.  The live music is very cool and I love the carnival booths.  There are different games at every booth and most of them have a prize.  Last year, there was a candy buffet and it was very yummy. I saw magicans, jump ropers, musicians, and comedy acts. There was a raffle also, giving away prizes as big as tickets to Lagoon!  The Summer Book Festival will be really great this year, considering it is at a different location.  This year it will be at the Public Safety Building and Courtyard – 475 S 300 E Salt Lake City.  It will be held on May 30th, 2015 from 12-4pm. Don't miss it. -Ellie De Groote
