Brandon Mull visits March 13

March 13, 2015

FIVE KINGDOMS: March 2015 Appearances!

MURRAY, UT

LAUNCH EVENT
Friday, March 13
Doors Open: 6:30 PM
Show Starts: 7:00 PM

COTTONWOOD HIGH SCHOOL
5715 South 1300 East, Murray, UT  84121

Features a free comedy show with DC Comedy including special guest authors Richard Paul Evans (Michael Vey), Chad Morris (Cragbridge Hall) and Brandon Sanderson (The Reckoners)!

Brandon Mull will introduce Five Kingdoms: Book 3 – Crystal Keepers!

Books will be available for sale and all authors will be signing their books after the show. Crystal Keepers will be available for sale exclusively at this event 5 days prior to the official on sale!
