Read.Graduate.Succeed Grant helping thousands of Utah kids

Read Today stopped by Midvalley Elementary to celebrate a renewed three year, three million-dollar grant that would supply tutors to students that need help with reading. The tutoring program will help thousands of Utah kids receive the mentoring they need to read at grade level. Governor Gary Herbert was in attendance and said reading is such an important part of education and it plays a huge factor in what we choose to do when we grow up. The better readers we are, the better jobs we can get and the more successful we’ll be.

Sep 2, 2016
Celebrating a great Night at the Bees

A big thank you to our readers for such a great Night at the Bees. Our Summer Reading Program was a huge success this year. Thousands of readers attended with their family in tow to collect some great prizes and enjoy a fun night out. Read Today’s Summer Reading Program is an education collaboration between Read Today, KSL, the Deseret News, The Governor’s Reading Program, The Salt Lake Bees, McDonald’s, Seagull Book and the Utah State Library. Kids who signed up for the program were tasked with reading 20 minutes a day during their summer break. Those who completed the challenge were rewarded with a pencil, a voucher for a free McDonald’s Happy Meal, a book and tickets to the Bees game. We had more readers in attendance than we ever had and hope to top that next year. If you missed out we would love to have you join our program next year. Just visit www.readtoday.com for more information about upcoming programs and reading challenges. Happy reading!
