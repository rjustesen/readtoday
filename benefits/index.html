Benefits

Read Today is an in-school tutoring program that benefits the following groups of our communities:


1) Students:

receive pride in progress, build an effective relationship with their mentor group, and reading becomes fun as they gain confidence though achieving goals.

2) Parents:

watch their child progress because the program works, see their confidence grow and their love for reading sky-rocket.

3) Teachers:

are unable to give the individualized attention every child deserves. This program enables the teacher to continue at the pace of the class, while not excluding those that are behind. Read Today uses the proven STAR method designed by the State Office of Education.

4) Volunteers:

take part in a grass-roots movement. While their maybe an initial worry about the 1-2 hour a week time commitment and competing demands, the payoff makes it worthwhile. You are truly making a difference in a child's life.

5) Business owners:

are giving back to their community while building future workforce.

Read Today has an 85% success rate, an on-site coordinator that provides training and records progress and real-time results that are truly remarkable. To join the movement, click here.
